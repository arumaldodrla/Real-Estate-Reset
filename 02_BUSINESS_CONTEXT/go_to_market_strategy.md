# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** Mid-market brokerages (5–20 agents) and independent transaction coordinators in the US and Canada.
- **Secondary:** Independent agents with small support teams.

## Launch Strategy (Months 1–3)

- **Beta Program:** Recruit 20–50 trusted real estate teams for a high-touch beta program. Offer a significant discount in exchange for feedback.
- **Content Marketing:** Launch a blog and YouTube channel with content focused on real estate operations, productivity, and technology.
- **Partnerships:** Establish initial partnerships with 2–3 real estate coaching organizations and MLS providers.

## Growth Strategy (Months 4–12)

- **Paid Advertising:** Launch targeted ad campaigns on Google and Facebook, focusing on keywords like "real estate transaction coordinator software" and "real estate CRM."
- **Webinars:** Host monthly webinars on topics of interest to the target audience.
- **PR Push:** Engage with real estate technology publications and influencers to generate buzz.
- **Referral Program:** Launch a referral program that rewards existing customers for bringing in new business.

## Customer Acquisition Channels

| Channel | % of New Customers | CAC | Scale |
|---|---|---|---|
| Organic (SEO, word-of-mouth, referrals) | 40% | $40 | High |
| Content marketing (blog, YouTube, webinar) | 20% | $60 | Medium |
| Paid ads (Google, Facebook) | 20% | $120 | High |
| Partnerships (MLS, associations) | 10% | $150 | Medium |
| Direct sales (Enterprise) | 10% | $200 | Low (high value) |
