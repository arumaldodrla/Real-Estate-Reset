# Business Model & Pricing

## Revenue Model: Subscription + Usage-Based Hybrid

### Tier 1: "Transaction Coordinator" – $300/month

**Target Customer:** Single coordinator or small team (2–5 agents)

**Features:**
- Unlimited transactions
- Document management (Smart Inbox, auto-categorization)
- Deadline tracking & reminders
- eSignature integration (DocuSign)
- Party portals (magic links)
- Email support
- 2 team members

**Conversion Path:**
- 14-day free trial
- 15–20% of trials convert to paid

### Tier 2: "Team + CRM" – $500/month

**Target Customer:** Brokerage with 5–10 agents

**Features:**
- Everything in Tier 1
- Agent Command Center (pipeline, commissions, reports)
- Light CRM (contacts, notes, follow-ups)
- Up to 5 team members
- Priority email/chat support
- Integrations: Zoho CRM, Authorize.net

**Conversion Path:**
- Upgrade from Tier 1 after 2–3 months of usage
- 20–30% of Tier 1 teams upgrade within Year 1

### Tier 3: "AI Property Agent" – $800/month + $50/1000 messages (overage)

**Target Customer:** Brokerage with 10–25 agents wanting customer engagement automation

**Features:**
- Everything in Tier 2
- AI Property Chatbot (WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, website widget)
- MLS/API property syncing (RETS, Zillow, Redfin)
- Vector embedding & semantic search
- Multi-language support (Spanish, Portuguese, Mandarin)
- Up to 10 team members
- Dedicated account manager
- API access for custom integrations

**Overage Pricing:**
- Base: 10,000 messages/month included
- Overage: $50 per 1,000 additional messages
- Typical overage: 5K–20K extra messages/month ($250–1,000)

**Conversion Path:**
- Upsell to Tier 2 customers after 6–12 months
- 25–40% of Tier 2 teams upgrade to Tier 3 within Year 2

### Tier 4: "Enterprise" – Custom Pricing

**Target Customer:** Large brokerages (50+ agents, regional/national)

**Features:**
- Everything in Tier 3
- Unlimited team members
- Unlimited chatbot messages (monthly fee)
- White-label chatbot (custom domain, branding)
- SLA guarantees (99.9%+ uptime)
- Custom integrations (existing brokerage systems)
- Dedicated support + quarterly business reviews

**Pricing Model:**
- Minimum: $2,000–5,000/month
- Annual contracts with volume discounts
